about

I'm Quan — a sound engineer and post-production specialist based in Melbourne, Australia. I'm planning to move to Toronto, Canada in mid 2026 to explore new work opportunities and expand my horizons.

My work spans film and television post-production, music mixing and mastering, and sound design. I've delivered audio for major independent network television, independent films, and music projects across multiple genres including rock, hip-hop, and electronic.

I hold a Diploma of Sound Production from RMIT University (2022) and have experience working with industry-standard tools including Pro Tools, Ableton Live, Logic, and DaVinci Fairlight. My workflow integrates analog emulation (UAD), precision processing (FabFilter, Waves), and strict adherence to broadcast standards (ATSC A/85, streaming optimization).

Outside of client work, I produce experimental pop/R&B and electronic music under the name [REDACTED, for now...]. Beyond the studios, dancefloors, and concert halls, you can find me winding down beside a punching bag practicing my Muay Thai, or in the gym, trying to hit a heavy PR. The balance between work, life, and self-improvement in all facets is what gets me motivated every day.
